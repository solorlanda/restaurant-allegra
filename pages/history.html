About us

Our restaurant opened its doors in 1997, being one of the first Italian food restaurants in Denmark, always run as a family more than as a company, it has remained in the market over the years and has been nominated for Michelin awards. on three occasions.
We are a kitchen team, the kitchen team is a group of individuals who collaborate to prepare and serve food in a culinary establishment. This team typically consists of various roles, such as chefs, sous chefs, line cooks, prep cooks, and kitchen assistants. Each member plays a crucial role in the smooth operation of the kitchen, from prepping ingredients to cooking dishes to ensuring food safety and cleanliness. Effective communication, coordination, and teamwork are essential for the kitchen team to deliver high-quality meals efficiently and consistently. Additionally, a positive work environment, mutual respect, and a shared passion for food contribute to the success of the kitchen team.
